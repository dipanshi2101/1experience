What is 1experience ?

A distributed HyperLedger based Qualification distribution System. All academic documents, Certifications, and industry experience in one place making it easier to manage and track all your documents. Huge advantage for hiring and education institutions to manage and verify the documents of any applicant.

1experience - Individual Perspective

Tired of collecting papers? Here is a solution to recieve all you acedemic and industry related doscuments in one place within few minues.

1experience - Organisation perspective

Tired of verifying the authenticity of documents provided by applicant? Here is a solution to verify all your documents in one place within few minues.
